Jojo's Bizzare Adventure

Jojo's Bizarre Adventure is a multi-part series about a cursed bloodline. The inheritors of this bloodline is the Joestar family. This family must fight the forces of evil to keep their bloodline safe.

Jotaro Kujo

Jotaro is a fictional character from the Japanese manga JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, and the protagonist of Series 3. He has a recognizable attire of trenchcoat-length school uniform jacket with yellow chains on the collar.

Dio Brando

The central villain of the Jojo's Bizarre Adventure series, Dio is an immortal vampire and the arch rival of the Joestar clan.
